Our Story

French craft, West Texas warmth.

Paris Croisserie began with a simple idea: that the unhurried pleasure of a Parisian café belongs everywhere — even on Milwaukee Avenue. Each day starts before dawn, laminating dough by hand and pulling shots of espresso so that Lubbock can slow down, even if only for one perfect croissant.
